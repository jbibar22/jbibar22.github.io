About James on Main

Refined local dining with creative flavor and memorable presentation

James on Main is a standout New American restaurant in Hackettstown known for creative dishes, fresh ingredients, and beautifully presented plates. From intimate dinners to special celebrations, guests consistently praise the restaurant’s warm atmosphere, memorable service, and exceptional flavor in every course.
